About Batman

Batman is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. Created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, the character first appeared in Detective Comics #27, in 1939.

History

Since his first appearance, Batman has been featured in various media including films, television series, and video games. His character has evolved over the decades, reflecting changes in society and culture.

Batman in Media

Batman has appeared in numerous films, TV shows, and video games. From the classic 1960s TV series to the critically acclaimed Dark Knight trilogy, Batman remains a cultural icon.
